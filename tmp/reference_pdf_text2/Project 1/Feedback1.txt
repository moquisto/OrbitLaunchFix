===== PAGE 3 =====

Project feedback
•Accuracy of integrators: Euler O(Δt), Velocity Verlet 
O(Δt2), RK4 O(Δt4)
•But only VV is symplectic
•So RK4 is more accurate over short times
•For Hamiltonian dynamics VV is more accurate for 
energy conservation over long times (but can have 
worse accuracy)
•Verlet does not support velocity dependent acceleration
•Some students saw bad energy conservation with RK4 
over 30 steps, either too fast drop of energy or an 
increase; likely cause: a bug in the code
