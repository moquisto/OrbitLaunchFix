===== PAGE 4 =====
Feedback traffic model 2.2 b)
•
2.2 b) “how many simulations to get 0.001 accuracy”
•
When averaging the flow rate over 100 steps you need
~60-120 simulations
•
When taking the final step flow rate only you need
~2500 simulations
•
Thus 20-30x better statistics when using the average!
•
Both answers are right and this actually provides insight:
•
The flow rate has a rather short correlation time of
about 4 time steps
