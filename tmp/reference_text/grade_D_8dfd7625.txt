The effect of h
Here, we want only to highlight that the size of parameter h is very import, as bigger is,
less precision, but faster is the simulation, while if h is very small, we can obtain a high
precision, but then, it takes much more time. For this reason, in this simulation it has
always been consider h=0.5 cm as it is a balanced value.
